PRAVRITTI 2024
Pioneering Paths

9th- 10th March, 2024


PRAVRITTI is the annual career festival organized by the Office of Career Services (OCS) at IIT Delhi. It aims to bridge the gap between students and the professional world by providing a platform for networking, learning, and exploring career opportunities.
   
     Non-recruiting events are open to general public.

Job and Internship Fair

Our event aims to provide a platform for students and job seekers to connect with employers from diverse industries and explore job opportunities. Open to only IIT Delhi students, This will facilitate the conventional hiring processes in a manner, which helps both companies and students in choosing a more suited job role. Interested companies can register below.

Campus Ambassador Programme

Are you looking to enhance your professional skills and connect with industry experts? Look no further than Pravritti, the annual career festival at IIT Delhi!. Pravritti brings together students and industry leaders to network, share knowledge, and explore career opportunities.

Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to be a part of Pravritti, the premier career festival at IIT Delhi. Apply now to be a Campus Ambassador and join us in creating an unforgettable experience for students and professionals alike!

CorpComp

Join us for exciting competitions where you'll work in teams to develop innovative solutions to real-world challenges. With access to cutting-edge tools and experienced mentors, you'll showcase your skills and creativity while making meaningful connections with peers and professionals. Compete for a chance to win prizes and recognition for your work.

Workshop Wave

A career-oriented workshop that provides professionals with the opportunity to enhance their skills and knowledge. Attendees will have the chance to learn new techniques and strategies, network with peers and industry leaders, and gain valuable insights into their chosen profession.Whether you are looking to advance your career or explore new opportunities, Workshop Wave has the perfect workshops for you.

Leadership Talks

Join us for a series of inspiring leadership talks featuring accomplished leaders from diverse fields. Gain valuable insights and practical tips for developing your own leadership skills, and connect with other like-minded professionals. Don't miss this opportunity to learn from successful role models and achieve your leadership goals.

Panel Discussions

Join us for a panel discussion on career development featuring accomplished professionals from diverse fields. Gain valuable insights and practical tips on navigating your career path, building your skills, and achieving your professional goals. This interactive session includes Q&A and covers networking, job searching, personal branding, leadership development, and more.

Human Library

Dive deep into the wealth of experiences shared by distinguished alumni of IIT Delhi through our Human Library. Immerse yourself in engaging conversations and stories that offer invaluable insights into various career paths and industries. Whether you're considering different career options or seeking guidance on navigating the professional world, our alumni are here to share their wisdom and experiences. Explore diverse perspectives, gain practical advice, and make informed decisions about your future endeavors.

Mock Interviews

Prepare for success in your future job interviews with our Mock Interviews program. Practice your interview skills in a supportive environment and receive constructive feedback from experienced students who have excelled in the interview process. Our mock interviews simulate real-world scenarios, allowing you to refine your communication, problem-solving, and interpersonal skills. Whether you're a seasoned interviewee or new to the process, our tailored feedback and personalized coaching will help you approach interviews with confidence and poise. Gain valuable insights, identify areas for improvement, and elevate your interview performance to stand out to potential employers.
